# KaryoTME (Karyotype-driven modulation of the Tumor Microenvironment) 
A multi-omics pan-cancer pipeline to identify key somatic copy number alterations (SCNAs) driving cancer immune evasion 

This study utilizes data from TCGA and CPTAC to study gene, cytoband, and chromosome specific aneuploidies in relation to immune evasion in cancer. We also incorporated single-cell RNA Sequencing data to validate large-scale cancer genomics findings, survival data from MSK-IMPACT, and survival analysis of clinical data obtained from Caris Life Sciences.
